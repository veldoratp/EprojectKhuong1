“Music Night in the Clouds” at May Lang Thang

2015

For a long time, the city of Da Lat has become a symbol of romance and dream, and the Wandering Clouds is no exception to this rule. May Lang Thang is a combination of homestays, cafes, restaurants and even pubs. But most young people who love music, or love Da Lat, know about May Lang Thang through 'music nights in the clouds'. A feature of these concerts is that they are held at 16:30 - a beautiful time. The most beautiful day of the day with 'clouds hanging overhead and sunshine overhead' surrounded by mountains and hills, with golden sunshine and pink clouds. The 'in the clouds' stage since its inception has gathered the hottest names in the village. Vietnamese music such as Phan Manh Quynh, Trung Quan, Nguyen Ha, Vu Cat Tuong, Thuy Chi, Ha Nhi, Vu, ... The space is bold in the mountains and hills of Da Lat with a panoramic view of the city and the entrance road. the winding, precarious part, but that's also why 'Music Night in the Clouds' brings unique experiences to the audience. When the coolness of Da Lat's atmosphere mixes with lively melodies and live-band will be make you have a relaxing and peaceful experience when coming to this place.

Soul of the Forest at Flamingo Dai Lai

2016

True to its poetic name, Soul of the Forest is held in the middle of a pine forest, within the campus of Flamingo Dai Lai Resort - Top 10 most beautiful resorts in the world in Vinh Phuc, about 45 minutes away from Hanoi. With the main concept Inspired by the romantic and mystical pine forest, Soul of the Forest focuses on the audience's connection with the singer and with the music. Under the pine trees, the stage is designed in a rustic style, along with an arc-shaped seating area to create a feeling of warmth and closeness. The guests of the night are famous names such as Ha Tran, Le Quyen, Uyen Linh, Bang Kieu, Le Hieu, Lan Nha, Bui Lan Huong,... Soul of the Forest have been telling audiences stories of music that move their souls. Not only being immersed in the tunes in a relaxing space, the audience will also enjoy the impressive works of art in the collection of the Flamingo Museum of Contemporary Art. The combination of nature - music - art at Soul of the Forest will definitely be an unforgettable experience.

American Idol

2002

American Idol is an American music competition and reality television show. The contest began to be held on June 11, 2002 and is one of the most popular television programs in the United States. American Idol is licensed from the British show Pop Idol, founded by producer Simon Fuller. American Idol was founded with the purpose of finding the most promising singers in the United States through a series of auditions held around the country. After that, the finalists will be decided by the audience's vote by phone. One feature of the program is that after the contestants' performances, the judges will give their own comments to help the audience orient the contestant they voted for. American Idol contestants must be able to sing and not currently have any recording contract or be under the management of a music organization. They must be US citizens or foreigners working in the US for a long time. American Idol is the starting point for many young singers to achieve success in their later careers.

Best song

2016

Sing My Song is a reality TV show that searches for versatile artists who can compose and sing well. The program is organized with the aim of finding, fostering and developing generations of multi-talented musicians and singers (artists). The contestants selected for the recording round will be supported by the Organizing Committee in ability training, product release, image building and media sponsorship. The program has been distributed to more than 30 countries around the world and in Asia, first being broadcast in China with an overwhelmingly high rating and overwhelming compared to The Voice of China. In 2016, the program was copyrighted by Cat Tien Sa Company with the name Best Song and broadcast in Vietnam on channel VTV3.The format of this exam is similar to the Hidden Face round of the Vietnamese vocal program.Eight contestants of each team were divided into four pairs by lottery, each pair of two contestants composing the same theme of the album and sharing a separate room within 24 hours.The contestant who gets 16 votes first will become the first season's Best Song Champion.
